Air Fryer Pizza Rolls

Description

One of the things I loved as a kid was pizza rolls. Do you? I cant eat them these days thanks to being gluten-free, but the rest of my family loves them.

Ingredients

  • 1 Package Totino's Frozen Pizza Rolls
  • Olive Oil Cooking Spray

Steps

  1. Spray the basket of the air fryer with olive oil cooking spray.
  2. Place an even layer of frozen pizza rolls in the basket.
  3. Add another coat of olive oil cooking spray on top of the pizza rolls.
  4. Air Fry at 390*F for 8 minutes, I suggest checking on them at 6 minutes since all air fryers heat differently.
  5. Once done, carefully remove and serve.
